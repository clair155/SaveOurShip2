Save Our Ship 2 is an unofficial expansion for RimWorld. Build a liveable spaceship and continue your colonists' stories in orbit - engaging in ship-to-ship combat with pirates, searching derelicts for treasure, and perhaps even discovering a phenomenal cosmic secret or two! SoS2 includes two custom endings: build the Johnson-Tanaka drive to transport your colonists to a different rimworld for a New Game+ experience, or evolve one your colonists to machine godhood using the Archotech Spore.

Save Our Ship 2.8 Tactical Update
Featuring new accuracy system:
- Ships can now completely dodge enemy shots. Small and fast ships have much better chances to dodge than large slow ships.
- Dodge chance depends hugely on Thrust to Weight Ratio. Large cruisers won't dodge, but small fighters will.
- Now there is use for manning pilot console in battle! Pilot intellectual skill will increase dodge chance.
- Dodge chance also depends on weapon range. It is much easier to dodge railgun shots than cannon shots.
- For shots that weren't dodged completely, weapon accuracy now varies greatly. Mechanics are similar to dodge chance.
- There is Odyssey integration for new mechanics. Pilot Assistant on player ship pilot will give dodge bonus.
- Odyssey gravship can land in SOS 2 space, can be built/upgraded there too. Vacuum gear, implants, genes are also compatible.
- Odyssey compatibility is not final yet.

Requires Harmony (https://github.com/pardeike/Harmony) and Vehicle Framework (https://github.com/SmashPhil/Vehicle-Framework).
